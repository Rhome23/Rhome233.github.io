Cat Feeding Methods

01

Meal Feeding Cats

Meal feeding means you're providing food to your cat only at specific meal times during the day. Both canned and dry foods can be fed in this manner.

02

Free Feeding Cats

When you free feed your cat, their food is available at all times. Keep in mind that only dry foods can be fed in this way because wet food should not be left out throughout the day.

03

Combination Feeding

Mixed feeding can be defined as serving canned food as a twice-daily meal and dry food is freely available.

Choose a Litter Box

01

Get the Right Size Litter Box

Full-size boxes may be too big and intimidating for a small kitten.

02

Provide More Than One Litter Box

At a minimum, there should be one more litter box in your house than the number of cats.

03

Uncovered versus Covered Litter Boxes

Many cats prefer to use an uncovered box.
